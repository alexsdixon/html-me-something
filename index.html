MY SOCCER JOURNEY

2018 SOCCER PLAYER OF THE YEAR

American Soccer League Exclusive Interview with the World's Best


Cristiano Ronaldo started playing soccer when he was 7 years old. The striker started out by playing for his school.

During his first school championship he gained attention from several scouts and was later signed to Rockfort FC.

In his first match for the club , he came on to the field as an substitute and scored 2 goals against ST.Louis All stars.

Ever since then he started every game and finished his first season for Rockfort FC with 28 goals and 17 assist.

Even though they didn't win the league they managed to earn the second place trophy and at the age of 21 Cristiano was signed to Real Madrid

Cristiano's main position on the field is a Striker but his coach sometimes play him on the wing for his creative passes

During his time at Real Madrid the club has won 21 trophies across multiple competitions and the player himself has won several individual awards.

Last season he scored 48 goals and had 31 assist and that made him receive the Player of the Year Award

Message from the Star

I want to thank my fans and coach for all their support

I would also like to thank my teammates and hope we continue to win more Trophies
