My Soccer Journey

2018 Player of the Year

My name is Cristiano Ronaldo.I started playing Soccer when i was 7 years old

The first team that i played for was Rockfort FC. I scored my first goal in my opening match

My favorite position on the field is a Striker but my coach likes to make me winger

I'm now 24 years old and play for Real Madrid FC

Last season i scored 48 goals and had 31 assist and that made me receive the Player of the Year Award

I want to thank my coach and team mates for all their support
